Invisalign

Have you always wanted a great smile without the metal look? Well, thanks to Invisalign, now you can have that smile! Invisalign treatment offers patients an alternative to conventional metal braces. Clear, custom fitted removable aligners are worn and every four to six weeks you will be re-examined to evaluate your progress. On average the total treatment time is approximately one year. Your new smile is just a phone call away!
